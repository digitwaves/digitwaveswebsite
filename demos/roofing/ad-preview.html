The Anatomy of Your Ad

1. The Visual (Background): This is your `instagram-ad-1.mp4`. It runs continuously to show the premium aesthetic of your work.

2. The Hook (Center Text): This text is placed directly over the video. People read this first before they read the caption. It highlights a pain point (storms).

3. The Caption (Bottom Left): Explains the value proposition briefly.

4. The Call to Action (Gold Button): This is what you configure in Meta Ads. When clicked, it drives them directly to the `index.html` landing page we built.

To use this for real, you would use a video editor (like CapCut) to add the Hook text directly onto your mp4 file, then upload it to Meta/Instagram Ads Manager.
