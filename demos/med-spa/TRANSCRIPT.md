# Med Spa Demo Transcript

Date: May 12, 2026

## Project Prompt

Create a premium med spa website demo for DigitWaves.

Goal:
Build a realistic fictional med spa website concept that feels like a live, polished business website, while still being used as a DigitWaves portfolio/demo asset. The site should help show how DigitWaves builds high-trust websites and AI-enabled customer experiences for high-profit local service businesses.

Demo Business:
Use a fictional premium med spa / aesthetic clinic brand. The site should feel upscale, calm, trustworthy, and consultation-first. Do not use fake real-world addresses, fake phone numbers, fake provider credentials, fake testimonials, fake reviews, or fake before-and-after claims.

Suggested Brand Direction:
A modern aesthetic clinic offering services like Botox, dermal fillers, skin rejuvenation, facials, and body contouring. The tone should be professional, elegant, clear, and reassuring.

Main URL:
digitwaves.com/demos/med-spa/

Primary Purpose:
Show how a med spa website can:
- Create trust quickly
- Explain high-ticket services clearly
- Guide visitors toward consultation requests
- Support local SEO structure
- Answer common customer questions
- Prepare for an AI-enabled treatment guide or lead intake flow

Hero Direction:
Use a full-page hero with an upscale aesthetic consultation image. The image should show a professional provider and adult client in a modern med spa consultation setting, reviewing treatment options on a tablet or clipboard. No needles, no procedure, no visible medical claims, no logos, no text in the image. Leave negative space for headline overlay.

Hero Copy:
Headline:
A Modern Med Spa Website Built to Turn Interest Into Consultations

Subheadline:
A premium website concept for aesthetic clinics that need clearer services, stronger trust signals, and a smoother path from treatment research to booking.

Primary CTA:
Book a Consultation

Secondary CTA:
Explore Services

Important Framing:
The page should not feel like an obvious "demo" while someone is viewing it. It should feel like a real premium med spa site. However, do not deceive viewers into thinking this is an actual operating clinic. On DigitWaves portfolio/work context, it can be labeled as a concept website or sample build.

Recommended Pages:
- Home
- About
- Services
- Botox & Wrinkle Relaxers
- Dermal Fillers
- Skin Rejuvenation
- Contact / Consultation Request

Homepage Sections:
1. Full-page hero
2. Popular services preview
3. Trust and consultation-first positioning
4. Why clients choose this clinic
5. AI-enabled treatment guide preview
6. Service education section
7. Consultation CTA

Services To Include:
- Botox & Wrinkle Relaxers
- Dermal Fillers
- Skin Rejuvenation
- Facials & Skin Health
- Body Contouring

Trust Signals:
Use realistic but non-deceptive trust elements:
- Consultation-first care
- Personalized treatment planning
- Natural-looking aesthetic goals
- Clear treatment education
- Modern, comfortable environment
- Safety-focused language
- Provider-guided recommendations without naming fake credentials

Avoid:
- Guaranteed results
- Fake testimonials
- Fake reviews
- Fake before/after photos
- Fake doctors or certifications
- Medical diagnosis
- Claims that imply guaranteed treatment outcomes
- Overly generic agency language

AI-Enabled Website Angle:
Include a section showing how an AI treatment guide could help visitors ask common questions before booking. It should answer general education questions and guide visitors toward consultation, not diagnose or recommend treatment.

Example AI Questions:
- What is the difference between Botox and filler?
- Which treatments are commonly used for fine lines?
- What should I ask during a consultation?
- Is there downtime after skin rejuvenation?
- How do I know which service to book first?

CTA Strategy:
For the fictional clinic experience, use med spa-style CTAs like:
- Book a Consultation
- Explore Services
- Start With a Consultation
- View Treatment Options

For DigitWaves-facing portfolio/work pages, use agency CTAs like:
- Build a Site Like This
- Plan My Med Spa Website
- Request a Website Review

Design Style:
Upscale, modern, clean, calm, high-trust. Use warm neutrals, soft white space, refined typography, subtle teal or aqua accents to keep it compatible with DigitWaves styling. The site should feel premium but not flashy.

Implementation:
Start as a WordPress/Elementor demo page under:
digitwaves.com/demos/med-spa/

Later, create a separate Next.js/Vercel interactive AI demo under:
demos.digitwaves.com/med-spa-ai

The WordPress page sells the premium website foundation. The Next.js demo will demonstrate chatbot, lead intake, consultation prep, dashboard, and advanced AI workflows.
